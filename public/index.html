Hi, and welcome to my site!
I teach Statistics and Data Science at Cal Poly in beautiful San Luis Obispo, California. My current research interests include cross-disciplinary work in the Digital Humanities, methodologies for high-dimensional clustering, and Data Science education. My personal interests include travel, camping and backpacking, board games, and the San Jose Sharks.

Here to learn?
Many of the course materials for my classes are free and open-source. I am also a Certified RStudio Trainer. Please contact me to inquire about private tutoring, workshops, or trainings.

Interested in my data science work?
Read about my latest research projects, or learn more about my R packages. Don’t hesitate to reach out with questions about my projects, or to submit feature requests on GitHub.
I offer data science consulting services, both as long-term contracted projects and as one time drop-in help.
